Habipush

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Habipush is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best habit tracking experience:

  • Habit names and descriptions that you create
  • Habit completion data and timestamps
  • Reminder preferences and notification settings

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Display your habits and track your progress
  • Send you reminders and notifications
  • Generate progress charts and statistics

Data Storage

All your habit data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal information on our servers or share it with third parties. Your data remains private and under your control.

Notifications

Habipush uses local notifications to remind you about your habits. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

No Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your habit data is yours and yours alone.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information stored on your device. However, please remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Habipush does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
