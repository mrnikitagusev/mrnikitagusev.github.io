Find answers to the most common questions about using Habipush.

Why am I not receiving notifications from Habipush?

Please check that notifications are enabled in the app settings, that the habit toggle is turned on, and that you have set up a correct reminder interval for your habit.

How do I mark a habit as completed directly from the notification?

You can either long-press the notification until response options appear, or swipe down on the notification if it just arrived to reveal the action buttons.

Can I change the reminder time after creating a habit?

Yes! Tap on any habit to edit not only the reminder interval, but all other settings as well.

Use the "Contact Support" button below to send us an email. We'd love to hear from you!
